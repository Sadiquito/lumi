# Lumi - Design Guidelines (Updated)

## Visual Style

- **Font:** Inter (primary typeface)
- **Primary Background:** #121212 (deep space black)
- **Primary Accent:** #FF6B6B (sunset coral red)
- **Secondary Accent:** #FFD93D (sunset gold)
- **Tertiary Accent:** #4ECDC4 (calm aquamarine)
- **Surface Backgrounds (Cards/Containers):** #1F1F1F (charcoal gray)
- **Text Primary:** #FFFFFF (white)
- **Text Secondary:** #CCCCCC (soft gray)

## Page Layout Principles

- Mobile-first responsive design
- Cozy, card-based structure with generous whitespace
- Dark theme evoking a cosmic sunset, viewed from orbit
- Warm, reflective atmosphere rather than harsh sci-fi aesthetic
- Subtle animations for softness and flow
- Large CTAs for key actions (Journal Now, Schedule Call)

## Accessibility

- High-contrast text for legibility
- Large touch targets for mobile usability
- ARIA labels for screen readers
- Subtitles for AI-generated advice (post-call summaries)

## Brand Tone and Voice

- Calm, warm, inviting
- Trustworthy, non-intrusive
- Space-holder for personal reflection
- Gently supportive, non-judgmental
- Reflective coaching when invited

## Visual Inspirations

- Cosmic sunsets
- Distant planets on the horizon
- Night sky gradients
- Gentle auroras and space clouds
- Perplexity AI-inspired mood with more warmth and intimacy
